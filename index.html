Invite friends, family, or coworkers! Escape My Room is more fun with people you know. Each escape room can hold a maximum of 8 guests, but you can book as many or as few tickets as you like. If you don’t book all tickets, there’s a good chance someone else will book the remaining tickets for that timeslot and you’ll make new friends.

Buy tickets here on our website. All Escape My Room tickets must be purchased at least one hour in advance. Walk-ins are not allowed. Choose your room, select the amount of tickets you’ll need, and then find a date and a time that work well for you.

Get locked in a room! Show up to our location on time or risk forfiting your ticket. We cannot let you in if you are more than 15 minutes late. You have one hour to escape, if you do not escape within the hour, then we come in and show you what you missed.

While in the room, you must work together as a team to solve puzzles and clues in order to uncover the secrets of the DeLaporte family.

Come back! If you have a good time (and we promise you that you will), come back and experience a different room. A new escape room opens about every two to four months.

Still have questions? Look at our Tips and FAQs or email us at info@escapemyroom.com

Rooms

Escape My Room features rooms inspired by the DeLaporte mansion which was torn down to make way for a new hospital complex near the Superdome. Before the house was demolished, everything within it was removed and placed into storage.

In each escape room you will uncover mysteries involving the secretive DeLaporte family and their hidden role in shaping the history of New Orleans.

Mardi Gras Study

The year is 1990, and Odette DeLaporte has invited you to her study. As the last remaining heir in New Orleans to the DeLaporte fortune, she needs your help locating a treasure which went missing a long time ago.

The reclusive matriarch has not been seen in public for quite some time, yet she has agreed to meet with you with the hope that you can recover what’s been lost.

Jazz Music Parlor

Travel back in time to the scene of a murder in the music parlor of the DeLaporte Mansion. Join forces with the police and grieving DeLaporte family members as they comb the crime scene for evidence. Can the two groups cooperate long enough to solve this 60-year-old crime, or will the answers be lost forever?

The Jazz Parlor features relics of New Orleans’ most bizarre family, and numerous secrets untold. Expose the DeLaporte scandal as you learn the history of jazz and Mardi Gras Indians.

Buy Tickets

Use the calendar to purchase tickets, or open the calendar in a new window. Tickets cost $28 per person, and there are 7 tickets available for each time slot. If you would like more than one ticket, enter the number of tickets you would like to purchase before selecting a date.

If you have a code to redeem, please enter it on the first screen by clicking the button “redeem code”. Codes are given out when gift certificates are purchased. We do not currently offer any discounts on admission.

Location

Escape My Room is located in the U.S. Postal Service building on Loyola Avenue, between Girod and Julia Streets, one block from the Superdome and on streetcar line. Our building is within walking distance or a short cab ride from most major hotels. Specific details about how to enter the building will be provided upon booking.

Two hours of free parking are provided in the post office lot on a first come, first served basis.

Escape My Room does not allow walk-ins. Everyone visiting must have a ticket.
